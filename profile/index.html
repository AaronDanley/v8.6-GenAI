Profile.

Hello, I’m Aaron Danley, a multidisciplinary designer from St. Paul, Minnesota. Currently, I'm working for Slalom in Atlanta, Georgia.

For as long as I can remember, I've had an interest in technology. Some of my earliest memories are of playing with my older brother's I-99/4A. It was an amazing computer that opened my eyes to a world of endless possibilities.

Today, I design and build solutions for devices I could have only dreamed of as a child. I fully enjoy working on all aspects of this process, from planning and wireframing, to design and development. I feel lucky that I'm able to work in a field that allows me to solve new and unique problems every day.
